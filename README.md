# DEIB Working Group Issue Tracker
Welcome to the DEIB (Diversity, Equity, Inclusion, and Belonging) Working Group Issue Tracker! This repository, hosted on [GitHub](https://github.com/WordPress/DEIB-Issue-Tracker), serves as a central hub for coordinating DEIB initiatives within the WordPress community and beyond. Our goal is to foster a more inclusive, equitable, diverse, and welcoming environment for all contributors and participants.
## About the DEIB Working Group
The DEIB Working Group is committed to promoting diversity, equity, inclusion, and belonging within the WordPress community. We work on various initiatives, from outreach and training to policy development and community support. Our work is guided by the collective input and participation of community members who share our commitment to these values.
## How to Use This Repository
- **Issues**: We use issues as tasks or action items for DEIB initiatives. Browse existing issues to see what we're working on, or create a new issue to suggest an initiative or report a concern.
- **Discussions**: For deeper, threaded discussions on specific topics related to DEIB, we utilize the repository's Discussions feature. This is a great place to share ideas, ask questions, and engage with the community on DEIB matters.
- **Code (Markdown Files)**: The Code section of this repository is used to work on and contribute content related to DEIB initiatives, primarily using Markdown (.md) files. This may include drafting policies, creating training materials, or compiling resources.
## Getting Involved
We welcome contributions from everyone who shares our commitment to DEIB. Here are a few ways to get involved:
1. **Contribute to an Existing Issue**: Check out our [Issues](https://github.com/WordPress/DEIB-Issue-Tracker/issues) page to find tasks that need help. Feel free to comment on an issue to offer your assistance or provide input.
2. **Start a Discussion**: Have an idea or question related to DEIB? Start a new discussion on our [Discussions](https://github.com/WordPress/DEIB-Issue-Tracker/discussions) page to engage with the community.
3. **Contribute Content**: Interested in creating or improving DEIB-related content? Review our [CONTRIBUTE.md](https://github.com/WordPress/DEIB-Issue-Tracker/blob/trunk/CONTRIBUTE.md) guide for details on how to submit content through pull requests.
